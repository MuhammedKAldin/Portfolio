Privacy Policy

In short (and plain English), we are good people. We will never use your private information in a harmful way and never email you without your consent. We also dislike sites and companies that do that, and we respect your privacy, and clean inboxes! Below is the detailed privacy policy that based on our googling, we believe we must include, which may be updated here from time to time.



1. Protecting your privacy
We dont share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We dont engage in cross-marketing or link-referral programs with other sites. We dont send you unsolicited communications for marketing purposes. Account information is password-protected. Keep your password safe. Passwords stored in our database are encrypted. We cant access or retrieve your password on your behalf (you have to use the "Forgot Password" function) Our Devlopers does not knowingly collect any information from persons under the age of 13. If Our Devlopers learns that a posting is by a person under the age of 13, Our Devlopers will take all reasonable action to remove their posts, comments, and disable the account. Our Devlopers, or people who post or comment on Our Devlopers, may provide links to third party websites, which may have different privacy practices. We are not responsible for, nor have any control over, the privacy policies of those third party websites, and encourage all users to read the privacy policies of each and every website visited.

2. Protecting your privacy
We sometimes collect your email address, for purposes such as sending self-publishing and confirmation emails, authenticating user accounts, providing subscription email services, registering for forums, etc. Our Devlopers does not collect or store credit card information. We may collect personal information if you provide it in feedback or comments, post it on our forum, or if you contact us directly. Please do not post any personal information on Our Devlopers via comments or the forums that you expect to keep private. When you leave a comment your IP address is recorded in our database. This can help us combat SPAM and prevent abuse. Our web logs collect standard web log entries for each page served, including your IP address, page URL, and timestamp. Web logs help us to diagnose problems with our server, to administer the Our Devlopers site, and to otherwise provide our service to you.

3. Data we store
Our web logs and other records are stored indefinitely. Registered users can access and update their account information through their profile. Although we make good faith efforts to store the information in a secure operating environment that is not available to the public, we cannot guarantee complete security.

4. International Users
By visiting our web site and providing us with data, you acknowledge and agree that due to the international dimension of Our Devlopers we may use the data collected in the course of our relationship for the purposes identified in this policy or in our other communications with you, including the transmission of information outside your resident jurisdiction. In addition, please understand that such data may be stored on servers located in the United States. By providing us with your data, you consent to the transfer of such data.

5. Updates
Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time and all updates will be posted on this page.
